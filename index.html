Scuba Diving

"The sea, once it casts its spell, holds one in its net of wonder forever"
- Jacques Cousteau

Scuba diving is mainly done for the attraction of the unattainable undersea world. It is one area of nature that mankind has not been able to fully control, we simply are not able to breathe underwater. Hence, scuba diving gives us an opportunity to be in that underwater world, even if it is just for a limited amount of time. Of course, the underwater world is beautiful as well, with many people opting for scuba diving in Asia or scuba diving in the Red Sea or the Great Barrier Reef, said to be some of the worlds best scuba diving locations. The different colors and marine wildlife are so impressive in all these locations that people find themselves returning over and over again.
